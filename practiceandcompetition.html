some places where you can mark your coding skills

1.geeksforgeeks practice



GeeksforGeeks Practice is a platform where you can solve a wide range of programming problems to improve your coding skills. It offers: Interactive Problems: Practice coding problems across various domains like algorithms, data structures, and interview questions. Contests: Participate in regular coding contests to test your skills and compete with other programmers. Courses: Learn from industry experts through comprehensive online courses on topics like data structures, algorithms, and more. Job-A-Thon: Free hiring challenges where you can get placed in top companies. It's a great resource to enhance your coding intellect and prepare for interviews.



2.Hacker rank




HackerRank is a popular online platform designed to help programmers enhance their coding skills and prepare for technical interviews. Here's what it offers: Coding Challenges: A vast collection of coding problems across various domains like algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and databases. Competitions: Regular coding contests where you can compete with other programmers from around the world. Interview Preparation: Tools and resources to help you prepare for technical interviews, including mock interviews and coding tests. Learning Paths: Structured learning paths to guide you through different programming languages and concepts. Company Collaborations: Companies use HackerRank to evaluate and hire tech talent, making it a great platform to showcase your skills. It's a fantastic resource for both learning and demonstrating your coding abilities.



3.Top coder

TopCoder is a crowdsourcing platform that connects businesses with hard-to-find expertise. It offers a variety of services, including: Competitive Programming: Participate in algorithm competitions and Single Round Matches (SRMs) to test your skills against some of the best programmers in the world. Challenges: Solve real-world problems posed by companies, with winners receiving monetary compensation. Skill Building: Track your progress and improve your skills through various challenges and competitions. Community: Connect with like-minded individuals, share knowledge, and get help from other community members in forums. TopCoder is a great platform to enhance your coding skills, gain practical experience, and compete with other programmers.
